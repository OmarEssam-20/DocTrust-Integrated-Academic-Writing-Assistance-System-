Summarization:

The rapid advancement of artificial intelligence has transformed numerous industries, 
particularly in the domain of natural language processing. Modern transformer-based 
models such as BART and T5 have demonstrated remarkable capabilities in understanding 
and generating human language. These models are pre-trained on massive corpora and 
fine-tuned on specific downstream tasks, enabling them to perform abstractive 
summarization, paraphrasing, and semantic similarity computation. The DocTrust system 
leverages these state-of-the-art architectures to provide academic writing assistance, 
helping students and researchers improve the quality and originality of their work. 
By integrating summarization, paraphrasing, and plagiarism detection into a unified 
platform, DocTrust addresses the core challenges faced in academic writing and 
integrity verification.
